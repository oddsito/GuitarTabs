Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

left hand fingering (LHF) above and below tab      right hand fingering (RHF)
1 - index finger                                   p - thumb
2 - middle finger                                  i - index finger (forefinger)
3 - ring finger                                    m - middle finger
4 - little finger                                  a - ring finger (annular)
                                                   e - little finger
CII     barre on 2nd fret
CIV     barre on 4th fret
CVII    barre on 7th fret
CIX     barre on 9th fret

pp     - pianissimo - very soft
p      - piano - soft
mp     - mezzo-piano - medium soft
f      - forte - loud
mf     - mezzo-forte - medium loud
ff     - fortissimo - very loud
> > >  - decrescendo - becoming gradually softer
>      - accented, marked, stressed

Tripudiante         - in a dancing mood, with excitement
Allegro vivo        - lively, with vigour
non precipitoso     - without rushing
l.v.                - laissez vibrer (let vibrate) - let it ring
sempre              - always
tenere le posizioni - holding the position (LH)
sub.                - subito - suddenly
Ancora piu allegro  - even more lively
poco                - a little
rall.               - rallentando - slowing down
Tempo I             - return to the original tempo
rit.                - ritenuto - slowing down, suddenly slower
