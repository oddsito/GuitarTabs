Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
The Pepe Romero video has a different introduction to the sheet and comes
in at the start of measure 2 (video time 41secs) and then wanders off again
at measure 10, returning only for the rasgueado.
Neither Pepe Romero nor Viktor Sharii keep exactly to the sheet harmonics,
which appear to have been inconsistently notated. Some of the harmonics on
the 7th fret have been moved to the 5th or 12th frets. [comments welcome]
The grace notes on the 1st string are easier to play as mordents (as notated
in the tab - play the chord followed by the hammer and pull off).
