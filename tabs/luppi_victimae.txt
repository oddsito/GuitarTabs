Performance Notes

* The tempo is around 60 half notes per minute or 120 quarter notes per minute.

* This piece has no time signature, so I have marked notes from whole to
  thirty-second with W, H, Q, E, S, T. Breves (two whole notes) are marked B.

===    held note
g      grace note
p      pull-off
h      hammer-on
-      tenuto
.      staccato
>      marcato
(      arpeggiate
)
/.\    fermata

ff     fortissimo (very loud)
f      forte (loud)
mf     mezzo forte (medium loud)
mp     mezzo piano (medium soft)
p      piano (soft)
pp     pianissimo (very soft)
mfz    mezzo forzando (between mf and f)
sub.   subito (suddenly)
rall.  rallentando (slow down)
riten. ritenuto (immediately slow down)

stretto   quickening

poco        a little
piu         more
meno        less
sempre      throughout
pont.       play at the bridge (ponticello)
sul tasto   play over the fretboard
sub.        suddenly (subito)

<5>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 5th fret
<7>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 7th fret
<9>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 9th fret
<12>   natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 12th fret
[5]    artificial harmonic - play indicated fret, touch point on 17th fret
