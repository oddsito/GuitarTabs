Performance Notes
* From the sheet music:
  Focus on the chords, control of dynamics, and the right-hand attack, all with
  a minimalist aesthetic. This piece was inspired by Villa-Lobos, the minimalist
  studies of Pedro Julian Florez, as well as Steve Rich and his famous
  "Electric Counterpoint".

* The highest note of the chord in measure 12 is an F in the sheet music,
  but I think this is an engraving error.
