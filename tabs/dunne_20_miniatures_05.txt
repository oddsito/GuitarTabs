Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
"While I have indicated many of the fingerings I use, I invite guitarists
to explore other options. Similarly, I feel that interpretation, dynamics,
tempi, and phrasing should be guided not only by the composer but also by
the performer. I am often delighted when I hear a performance of my music
that includes new expressive ideas. The indicated tempi are suggestions,
not specific requirements." - Matthew Dunne

The tempo of the MIDI is 112 bpm (a little slower than given in the sheet).

left hand fingering (LHF) below the tab -
1 - index finger (forefinger)
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger (pinkie)

===    - note held, length of note
arp.   - arpeggiate - ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest note
bpm    - beats per minute
rit.   - ritenuto - slowing down, suddenly slower
tempo  - speed (a tempo - return to the original tempo)

c1     - part barre on the 1st fret
C1     - barre on the 1st fret
c2     - part barre on the 2nd fret
C2     - barre on the 2nd fret [etc]
