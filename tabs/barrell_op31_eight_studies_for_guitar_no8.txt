Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Left Hand Fingering (LHF) below the tab -
1 - index finger (forefinger)
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger (pinkie)

===    - note held, length of note, duration
>      - (above the tab) accented, marked, stressed
< < <  - (below the tab) crescendo - becoming gradually louder
> > >  - (below the tab) becoming gradually softer
accel. - accelerando - accelerating, becoming faster
arp.   - arpeggiate (ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest)
bpm    - beats per minute
cresc. - crescendo - becoming gradually louder
F.     - Fermata - pause, note(s) held
mf     - mezzoforte - moderately loud
mp     - mezzopiano - moderately soft
sf     - sforzando - forced, strongly accented, suddenly louder
t      - (above the tab) tenuto - stressed, slightly accented, played firmly,
         the note is held for at least its full length
