Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

left hand fingering (LHF) above and below tab      right hand fingering (RHF)
1 - index finger                                   p - thumb
2 - middle finger                                  i - index finger (forefinger)
3 - ring finger                                    m - middle finger
4 - little finger                                  a - ring finger (annular)

The fingering in measures 11 & 73 is taken from the sheet. It is very difficult
to play as written (holding the C on the 5th fret while playing the D on the
10th fret with the 3rd finger). Maybe the C could be played on the 4th string?

CI       barre on 1st fret

p          - piano - soft
pp         - pianissimo - very soft
f          - forte - loud
ff         - fortissimo - very loud
mp         - mezzo-piano - medium soft
mf         - mezzo-forte - medium loud

l.v.                     - laissez vibrer (let vibrate) - let it ring
chiaro                   - clear
Andante piuttosto lento  - rather slow tempo, quite slow
ben ritmato              - very rhythmic
profondo                 - low, deep, profound
sempre                   - always, still, continuing
sff                      - sforzando - suddenly very loud (applied to chord)
scuro                    - dark
dolce                    - sweetly, gently
cresc                    - becoming louder
decresc                  - becoming softer
rall                     - rallentando - slowing down
dim                      - diminuendo - diminishing, becoming gradually softer

<7>    - natural harmonic on the 7th fret

>   - accented, marked, stressed
'   - slight pause
F   - Fermata - pause, note(s) held
h   - hammer-on
p   - pull-off
