Notes & Legend
--------------
Does anyone have the David Russell and Blazej Sudnikowicz arrangements?
The three sheet editions referenced are similar, the main differences being
in the left hand fingering, transpositions of  bass notes an octave higher
or lower, and the occasional switiching of the upper and lower parts.
The mordents (in measures 12,14,15,27,29,30) are optional. They are from
the keyboard sheet and are not always shown in the guitar sheet editions.
The piece is often performed with a capo on the 1st fret.
The tempo of the MIDI file is 66 bpm (beats per minute - quarter notes).
There are no repeated sections in the MIDI.
