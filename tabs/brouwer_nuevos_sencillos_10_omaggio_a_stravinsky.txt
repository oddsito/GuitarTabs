Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

left hand fingering (LHF) above and below tab      right hand fingering (RHF)
1 - index finger                                   p - thumb

cIII     part barre with 1st finger on 3rd fret
cV       part barre with 1st finger on 5th fret

>            - accented, marked, stressed
cresc        - becoming gradually louder
p            - piano - quiet, soft
p sub        - piano subito - suddenly soft
cresc molto  - greatly increasing in volume
f come prima - loud, in the same manner as the first time
ossia        - alternative passage which may be played instead of the original
f molto      - forte molto - much louder
'            - slight pause (breathmark)
GP           - general pause (silence)

h      - hammer-on
p      - pull-off
