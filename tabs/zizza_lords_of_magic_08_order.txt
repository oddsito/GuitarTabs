xOrder - by Keith Zizza
From the hit 1997 video game Lords of Magic
Original Song: https://youtu.be/wTG3m9M1lYo
Transcribed by rmbuzard@alaska.edu

The song is played on a synth with a harpsichord effect, so I tried to capture
the sound and rhythm while keeping it realistic to play on guitar.
