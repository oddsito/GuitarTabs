In the sheet there are places where it seems the notes are wrong or the left
hand fingering is wrong, or both are wrong! It's likely the tab has mistakes.
There are difficult stretches, so feel free to explore alternative fingerings.

measures 5-7 - in the sheet the G# on the 4th string is a quarter note
               but it is hard to see how it can be held
measure 7 - in the sheet the G on the 3rd beat is a half note, but the E on
            the 4th beat and the last note B are both played on open strings
measure 7 - in the sheet the last note is Bb, but it is shown as being played
            on an open string - both sound discordant so take your choice!
measures 10-13 - in the sheet the lower notes of the intervals are quarter
                 notes which often conflict with the following note
measure 20 - in the sheet the 12th note is C# but the fingering indicates C

             of the chord on the 5th beat are half notes B & F shown played
             with a barre, which is is just about conceivable as written in the
             tab if there is a cross-barre with the 1st finger and a barre with
             the 2nd finger for the F# on the 6th beat, and a difficult stretch
