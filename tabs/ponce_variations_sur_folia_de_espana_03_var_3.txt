thanks to Philipp Haas for the following corrections (1 December 2022)
measures 6,9 - 3 x B (1st string, 7th fret) changed to Bb (1st string, 6th fret)

left hand fingering (LHF)
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

cIII   part barre on 3rd fret
cV     part barre on the 5th fret

p  - piano - soft, quiet
f  - forte - loud, strong

Lento   - at a slow tempo

h     - hammer-on
p     - pull-off
/     - slide up
\     - slide down
F.    - Fermata - pause, notes held
<12>  - natural (open) octave harmonic on the 12th fret
1[13] - artificial harmonic, play 1st fret, point to the 13th fret
3[15] - artificial harmonic, play 3rd fret, point to the 15th fret
5[17] - artificial harmonic, play 5th fret, point to the 17th fret

|-3-| - triplet  - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
