forte   f  - loud
piano   p  - soft

Adagio        - at a slow tempo
arp           - arpeggiate - ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest
Allegro vivo  - lively, brisk, vivacious

h    - hammer-on
p    - pull-off
g    - grace note (ornament) - extra note played immediately before the
       main note, played in measure 122 as a pull-off

|-6-| - sextuplet  - 6 notes played in the time of 4 notes
|-7-| - septuplet  - 7 notes played in the time of 4 notes
