Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

left hand fingering (LHF) above and below tab      right hand fingering (RHF)
1 - index finger                                   p - thumb
2 - middle finger                                  i - index finger (forefinger)
3 - ring finger                                    m - middle finger
4 - little finger                                  a - ring finger (annular)

CI       barre on 1st fret
CII      barre on 2nd fret
CIII     barre on 3rd fret
CV       barre on 5th fret
CVI      barre on 6th fret
CVII     barre on 7th fret
CVIII    barre on 8th fret
CX       barre on 10th fret

forte          f   - loud
piano          p   - soft
pianissimo     pp  - very soft
mezzo-forte    mf  - medium loud
fortissimo     ff  - very loud
fortississimo  fff - very very loud

Allegro moderato - moderately quick tempo
arp              - arpeggiate
dim              - gradually softer
sfz              - sforzando - sudden accent, suddenly with force
metalique        - near the bridge
pizz             - sounds muted by the right hand
Piu vivo         - more lively
cresc            - crescendo - becoming gradually louder

|-3-| - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
>     - accented, marked, stressed
h     - hammer-on
p     - pull-off
<12>  - natural harmonic on the 12th fret
