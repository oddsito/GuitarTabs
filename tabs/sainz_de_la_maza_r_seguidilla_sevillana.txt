a Eduardo Conde
*   *   *   *   *   *   *   *   *   *

and archetypal dances of Sevilla.

Audio 2: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Warry2gPElM (Juan Jose Saenz)

Tablature legend:

h     - hammer on
s     - slide
p     - pull-off
N.H.  - natural harmonics
-     - tenuto
       (1. in this case: play less loudly than surrounding accented notes)
       (2. generally: play the note at its full value or slightly longer)
>     - accented note
cres. - crescendo (gradually increase volume)
rit.  - ritenuto (temporarily slow down, even 1 or few notes)
p     - piano (soft)
f     - forte (loud)
mf    - mezzoforte (moderately loud)
        (p, f, mf - below tab bars)

F.    - fermata (pause, prolong the note duration)
L     - tied (sustained) note
arp.  - arpeggiated chord
c9    - part barre
C4    - full barre
other - explained in the tab
