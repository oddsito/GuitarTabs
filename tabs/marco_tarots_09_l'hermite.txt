Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

left hand fingering (LHF) above and below tab
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

CII    barre on 2nd fret
CIV    barre on 4th fret
CV     barre on 5th fret
CIX    barre on 9th fret
CX     barre on 10th fret

piano          p  - soft
mezzo forte    mf - medium loud
pianissimo     pp - very soft

arp.     - arpeggiate, ripple upwards from lowest note to highest note
dolce    - gently, sweetly
cresc.   - crescendo - becoming gradually louder
decresc. - decrescendo - becomming gradually softer
rall.    - rallentando - slowing down
F.       - Fermata - pause, notes held for longer than their value
a tempo  - return to the normal speed (after slowing down with rall.)
allarg.  - allargando - gradually slower and more stately, broaden the tempo
