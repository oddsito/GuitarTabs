Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
"Eight Easy Pieces for Guitar (dC 182) is a collection of short pieces intended
for beginners. The title, however, is rather misleading; the term 'easy' in this
case is to be understood in a strictly musical sense, the pieces are indeed
shorter and simpler than usual, but from a technical standpoint, the pieces
are by no means easy."
- Franco Margola's Chamber Works with Guitar - Federico Jes Bonacossa, 2009

Franco Margola did not use key signatures in his compositions, but Study 1
appears to start and end in the key of A minor.

The tempo of the Norbert Neunzling video and the MIDI is 84 bpm (quarter notes).
