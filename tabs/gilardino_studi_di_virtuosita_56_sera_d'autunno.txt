Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

left hand fingering (LHF) above and below tab      right hand fingering (RHF)
1 - index finger                                   p - thumb
2 - middle finger                                  i - index finger (forefinger)
3 - ring finger                                    m - middle finger
4 - little finger                                  a - ring finger (annular)

CI       barre on 1st fret
CII      barre on 2nd fret
CVI      barre on 6th fret
CVII     barre on 7th fret
CIX      barre on 9th fret
CXI      barre on 11th fret
CXII     barre on 12th fret

piano           p - soft
p scuro           - dark and soft
forte           f - loud
mezzo-piano    mp - medium soft
mezzo-forte    mf - medium loud
pianissimo     pp - very soft

Andante quasi adagio        - moderately slow
l.v.                        - laissez vibrer (let vibrate)
                            - let it ring, don't dampen the sound
sempre                      - always
tenere al massimo ogni nota - keep every note to the utmost
le due voci equilibrate     - the two voices (upper and lower) are balanced
in rilievo la voce interna  - in relief the inner voice
poco rall                   - a little slower
agitato                     - quickly, with agitation
rall                        - rallentando - slowing down
fino al                     - until
Tempo I                     - return to the original tempo
a tempo                     - in time
allontandosi senza rall     - moving away without slowing down

>    - (above the tab) accented, marked, stressed
<7>  - natural harmonic on the 7th fret
<12> - natural harmonic on the 12th fret
<19> - natural harmonic on the 19th fret
