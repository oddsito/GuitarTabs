Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Order of play: measures 1-34, 1-34, 35-37

measure 6 - in the sheet the 2nd interval is D-F# (not D-F, as in measure 5)

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger (forefinger)
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger (pinky)

===    - length of note, note held, duration
=-=    - let the note ring, don't dampen the sound
>      - accented, strongly stressed
^      - (measure 27) marcato - accented, marked
/      - (below the tab) slide the finger up to the next note
\      - (below the tab) slide the finger down to the next note
/      - (between the notes) (glissando) slide the note up
\      - (between the notes) (glissando) slide the note down
<12>   - natural (open) (octave) harmonic on the 12th fret
bpm    - beats per minute
F.     - (measure 37) Fermata - pause (before playing the next study)
h      - hammer-on
p      - (between the notes) pull-off

c1     - part barre on the 1st fret with the 1st finger
C1     - barre on the 1st fret with the 1st finger
c2     - part barre on the 2nd fret with the 1st finger
C2     - barre on the 2nd fret with the 1st finger
etc
