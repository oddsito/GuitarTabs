Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
"Torems for Guitar is a 4 movement piece based on sculptures by J Jimenez
Deredia. It was part of a CD project called 'Canto a la Vida' by guitarist
Carlos Castro which featured new music inspired [by] Deredia's works.
'Totems' are short pieces that describe the [stages in the] night life of
a bat, [from] the darkness of the night [returning which] leads [it] to
wake up and prepare [itself] for the flight which [provides] food and...
interaction... with [its] peers, until the dawn that returns [it] to [its]
hiding place... away from the daylight."

Each measure lasts approximately 5 seconds.

4 - little finger

<19>    - natural harmonic on the 19th fret

12[19]  - artificial harmonic, play 12th fret, point to the 19th fret
