Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
The MIDI tempo is 60 bpm (beats per minute - quarter notes).
The order of play is 1-8, 1-8, 9-16, 9-16, 17-24, 17-24, 1-16.

left hand fingering (LHF) below the tab -
1 - index finger (forefinger)
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger (annular)
4 - little finger (pinky)

===    - note held, duration, length of note
> > >  - decresendo - becoming softer
D.C.   - Da Capo - return to the beginning
dim.   - diminuendo - diminishing, becoming gradually softer
f      - forte - loud, string
Fin.   - end of the piece
mf     - mezzo-forte - moderately loud
p      - piano - soft, quiet
pf     - probably means play the first few notes softly then (suddenly) louder
